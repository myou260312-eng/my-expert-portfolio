Mdabul Extension

Private Machine-to-Machine (M2M) Authentication Broker

About the Extension

Mdabul is a dedicated browser extension designed for secure machine-to-machine application authentication. It allows you to manage your application tokens independently without relying on third-party middleware or external authentication proxies.

Core Functionality

  • Independent Auth0 Integration: Connects directly to your dedicated Auth0 tenant dashboard.
  • Local Credentials Store: Safely stores and manages your secure Domain, Client ID, and Client Secret locally within your isolated browser storage.
  • M2M Token Generation: Programmatically fetches and provisions OAuth2 tokens directly to your local application development environment.
